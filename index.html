Who are we?

A pair of students working towards being the best sound engineers and Acapella music vocalist.

What we enjoy

The one thing we enjoy the most having jam sessions, that is where we most explore the avenures of sound technology and we encouparate acapella music by singing.

The Analog Desk

We used the analog desk for the first semester of 2018, and we are confiden in terms of operating the desk and understanding the different functionalities of each component.

The Jamms

Our Jam sessions are a way of "escaping" the stresses of life. This kind of escape helps us to refocus and rejuvinates us. The songs we jam are most of the time created on the spot and thus we are not restricted by notes.
